CONTENT_FAQ_SUB_TITLE_HOW_READ_CHART=How should I read the graph of the wind ?
CONTENT_FAQ_HOW_READ_CHART_1=The horizontal values at the bottom of the graph indicate the minutes elapsed, the vertical values on the left of the graph indicate the speed of the wind. Thus, the graph is read from the left (the most recent value) to the right (the oldest value).
CONTENT_FAQ_SUB_TITLE_ARROW_DIRECTION=What is indicated by the arrows ?
CONTENT_FAQ_ARROW_DIRECTION_1=The arrows indicate the flow of the wind (the direction where the wind blows).
CONTENT_FAQ_SUB_TITLE_ARROW_DIRECTION_MAP_CHART=Why the arrow in the winds graphic is different from that on the map ?
CONTENT_FAQ_ARROW_DIRECTION_MAP_CHART_1=The datas in the graph are updated by the station while the map is updated by the server. There may therefore be a difference between the two information.
CONTENT_FAQ_SUB_TITLE_MISSING_ARROW_CHART=Why are missing arrows in the graph ?
CONTENT_FAQ_MISSING_ARROW_CHART_1=Sometimes stations don't transmit information. As we don't extrapolate values there is thus no arrow.
